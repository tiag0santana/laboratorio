Welcome

The Laboratório de Processamento da Informação Biológica (PIB) was created in 2001 by the professors Allan Kardec Barros and Edson Nascimento with the purpose to encourage and give origin to the biomedical engineering and the medical informatics in the state of Maranhão.

×

The research lines developed at PIB focus in the analysis of biological signals and medical imaging, and the development of portable equipments with low cost to medical diagnosis. The developed artifacts is intended to: (a) help the doctors generating prediagnosis; (b) applications of Telemedicine in rural areas hard to access, in locations with the lack of specialized professionals, and in situations of medical emergencies.

Currently PIB has in its research staff students of PhD, Masters and Bachelors, in distinct areas of knowledge such as mathematics, physics, computing and engineering, under the guidance of Allan Kardec and Ewaldo Santana.

PIB occupies an area of 50m² in the building of Centro de Ciências Exatas e Tecnológicas at the Universidade Federal do Maranhão, and has computer resources and electronic instrumentation of last generation.
